Solar System facts:
  • The distance between Jupiter and Venus is slightly larger than the distance between Jupiter and Saturn.
  • Jupiter is so big that all the other planets in the solar system could fit inside it.
  • Neptune takes 165 years to orbit around the sun.
  • Mercury and Venus are the only planets in the Solar System without moons.
  • Neptune winds can reach 1600 km/h.
  • The average temperature in Venus is 467C.
  • Mercury day side can get as hot as 427C. The night side can get as cold as -173C.
  • Mercury takes longer to spin around its axis than to spin around the sun. Therefore, day in Mercury is longer than a year.
